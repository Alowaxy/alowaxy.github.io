2-DOF Humanoid Wrist Prototype

Humanoid Robot Club - Individual Project

Overview: Designed to enable complex hand movements while minimizing the moment arm to reduce power consumption on shoulder motors.

Implementation: Utilized two linear actuators with internal locking mechanisms to prevent back-driving without constant power draw.

Challenge: Resolved a passive roll issue by designing a custom yaw-pitch connection piece in SOLIDWORKS to stabilize the end effector.

Handheld Contra-Rotating Fan

Currently engineering a handheld fan powered by a single motor. I designed a custom bevel gear transmission system in SOLIDWORKS to achieve synchronized counter-rotation for maximum airflow efficiency.

1-DOF Rigid Body Robot Finger

Kinematic Linkage Design

Overview: Designed a 1-DOF self-curling robotic finger mechanism comprised of 7 rigid body links. The goal was to achieve natural grasping motion using only pure rigid body linkages for structural durability.
